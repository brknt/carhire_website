Rental Agreement

1.   Under this contract, the lessor (CSE) leases the vehicle described in the front page (to be specified as an instrument in this agreement) under the terms of this contract and the lessee leases the car by accepting the conditions..

 2.   21 years 1 year driving license for EDMR, EDMD, EDAR, EDAD, HDMD groups, 25 years for CDAD, IDMD, IDAR IDAD, IFAR groups, 2 year driving license, JDAD, SDMD, SDAR, SDAD, SFAR, SFMD, SFAD GROUP 25 YEARS for the driver's license, FDAR, FDAD, PDAR, PDAD, LDAR, LVMD group vehicles for 27 years of age with a 3-year driving license can benefit from CSE's car rental services.

3.  The tenant must comply with the regulations of the highways and the applicable laws. The tenant is liable for expenses (punishment, vehicle connection, towing, etc.) in case of criminal proceedings due to the use of vehicles contrary to the laws and regulations

 4.    The tenant shall be liable for the criminal proceedings and for the damages (traffic tickets, expenses related to the connection and withdrawal of the vehicle subject to the rental and other expenses incurred as a result of the accident) to be applied as a result of the accidents caused by the violation of road traffic rules..

5.   Rental is at least 1 day (24 hours). The daily price is applied for short term rentals. In case of delay, for each additional hour, 1/3 of the applied fee and if the delay exceeds 3 hours, the full day fee will be charged..

6.   The tenant shall return and deliver the vehicle within the agreed day. For all extensions, YES will apply to OTO and get its approval. In the case of extensions without approval, the tenant's car has been accepted by the parties to the unlawful law

7.   At the beginning of the rental, vehicle users are required to present driver's licenses and credit cards available. Payments are made with a valid credit card at the beginning of the rental. 

8.   At the beginning of the rental, the temporary blockage process is applied in the amounts varying from the tenant's credit card depending on the group of vehicles. If the rental period is more than 1 month, the blocked amount is as much as the monthly rental fee. The tenant, over the blockage, additional rent day amounts, lack of fuel, damage, traffic fines, etc. accepts in advance that it will not object to the collection of the costs related to the lease. In the case of transactions related to traffic fines, the service fee shall be charged up to 20% (20%) plus VAT for each penalty amount up to a maximum of 100 TL. In vehicles, HGS (Fast Transition System) tenant will return to the office of the return with the addition of 18% service charge upon the return of the lease. The tenants using the OGS (Automatic Pass System) are liable to pay the leakage costs..

9.   If requested by the lessee, the following additional insurances which extend the coverage may be purchased;

-  Mini Damage Insurance covers the property losses (excluding tire, glass, headlamp damage) with the customer's written declaration and without the need for a police report. If the damage is higher than the cost of the Mini Damage Insurance, the customer is responsible for all damage

-  The cost of damage to tires, glass and headlamps not covered by the standard automobile insurance, is assured by tire, glass and headlamp fuse.

- The voluntary liability insurance provides a maximum of TL 250,000 against third parties in addition to the mandatory liability insurance.

-  Personal accident insurance guarantees the occupants and occupants within the insurance limits..
